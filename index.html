Our Vision

To be at the forefront of transforming mental health awareness and community wellbeing, creating environments where individuals prosper with confidence, purpose, and joy.

Our Mission

To advance mental health awareness through education, advocacy, and safe spaces for recovery. We nurture programs that build resilience, restore hope, and equip individuals to live with confidence and purpose.

Our Objectives

• Promote mental health awareness and reduce stigma

• Support vulnerable individuals through counselling & mentorship

• Raise and manage funds for impactful community programs

Focus Areas

• Mental health education

• Emotional wellbeing & resilience

• Community outreach programs

• Mentorship & empowerment

Founder’s Story

Founded by Lucia Barati Setshego, an award-winning public speaker and advocate, this foundation was born from lived experiences of hardship, resilience, and determination.

Despite facing emotional and social challenges growing up, her journey became a source of strength — inspiring a mission to help others overcome trauma and build meaningful lives.
