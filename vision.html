Our Story

A journey of resilience, healing, and purpose

Where It All Began

Luminary Love Foundation was born from a deeply personal journey shaped by adversity, resilience, and an unshakable desire to make a difference. What began as lived experience has grown into a mission to uplift, heal, and empower others.

The Founder’s Journey

From childhood, Lucia Barati Setshego faced challenges many would consider overwhelming — emotional hardship, social struggles, and limited resources.

Raised by her grandmother under difficult circumstances, she witnessed resilience firsthand. These experiences shaped her strength, while also exposing the deep need for emotional support and mental health awareness in communities.

Rather than breaking her, these challenges became the foundation of her purpose.

Turning Pain Into Purpose

Through perseverance, support from mentors, educators, and healthcare professionals, Lucia rose above her circumstances and excelled academically.

She gained recognition as an award-winning public speaker, using her voice to advocate for mental health awareness and inspire hope in others facing similar struggles.

Why This Foundation Exists

Luminary Love Foundation exists to remind people that their circumstances do not define them. It is a safe space for healing, growth, and rediscovery.

Through compassion, guidance, and support, the foundation helps individuals rebuild confidence, find purpose, and move forward with hope.

A Message of Hope

No matter where you come from or what you’ve faced, your story is not over. With the right support, healing is possible, growth is achievable, and joy can be rediscovered.
